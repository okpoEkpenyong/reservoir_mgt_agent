# Privacy Policy for Exzing Reservoir Management Agent

**Last Updated:** 23-03-2026

Exzing Technology Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Exzing Reservoir Management Agent ("the Service") via the Microsoft Azure Marketplace.

### 1. Information We Collect
*   **Account Information:** When you activate your subscription, we receive your name, email address, and Tenant ID via Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD) to manage your access.
*   **Reservoir Data:** Any data files (CSV, Excel, etc.) you upload to the Service are processed solely for the purpose of reservoir analysis. 
*   **Usage Data:** We may collect basic logs (IP address, browser type) to ensure the security and performance of the Service.

### 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
*   Provision and maintain your subscription.
*   Provide technical support and updates.
*   Perform the engineering calculations and AI analysis requested through the Agent interface.

### 3. Data Retention and Security
*   **Security:** Your data is hosted on secure Microsoft Azure infrastructure. We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit and at rest.
*   **Retention:** We retain your account information as long as your subscription is active. Uploaded engineering data is deleted upon your request or when the subscription is terminated.

### 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell your data. We only share information with Microsoft as required to maintain your Marketplace subscription and billing.

### 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
